This Privacy Policy explains how GIVOGROW handles information when you use Fish Sort: Relax Puzzle. We are committed to protecting your privacy — here's exactly what we do and don't collect.

1. Information We Collect

Fish Sort: Relax Puzzle does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or location directly. Our third-party advertising partner, Google AdMob, may automatically collect the following non-personal data:

  • Device type, model, and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • IP address (used for approximate region-based ad targeting)
  • Google Advertising ID
  • App activity and interaction data
  • App performance and crash diagnostics

Note: This data is managed entirely by Google AdMob and cannot be deleted through us directly.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected by AdMob is used exclusively to:

  • Display relevant advertisements within the app
  • Monitor and improve app stability and performance
  • Understand general usage patterns to enhance the puzzle experience

3. Third-Party Advertising — Google AdMob

This app uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob operates under Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of personalised advertising on Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third party beyond what Google AdMob requires for ad delivery.

5. Children's Privacy

Fish Sort: Relax Puzzle is rated for ages 3 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe and appropriate for all ages.

6. Data Security

All data handled by AdMob is encrypted in transit. We follow industry best practices to keep the app and its users safe.

7. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes implies acceptance.
