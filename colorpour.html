This Privacy Policy describes how GIVOGROW handles data when you use ColorPour – Sort Puzzle. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

ColorPour does not collect any personal information (name, email, location) directly. However, our third-party ad partner Google AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • IP address (for general location-based ad targeting)
  • Google Advertising ID
  • App activity and interaction data
  • App performance and crash data

Note: Collected data cannot be deleted as it is handled entirely by Google AdMob's systems.

2. How Data Is Used

  • To display relevant ads within the app
  • To analyze app performance and fix bugs
  • To improve overall user experience

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob for advertisements. AdMob collects and processes data as per Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of personalized ads on your Android device: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties beyond what is required by Google AdMob for ad delivery. No data is sold or traded.

5. Children's Privacy

ColorPour is rated for ages 3+. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for all ages.

6. Data Security

All data collected through AdMob is encrypted in transit. We follow best practices to ensure app security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
