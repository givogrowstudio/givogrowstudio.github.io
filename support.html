Frequently Asked Questions

🎮 Why is the app crashing?
Try restarting the app or clearing cache from Settings → Apps → [App Name] → Clear Cache. Email us with your device model and Android version if the issue continues.
📶 Does the app need internet?
Our games can mostly be played offline. An internet connection is needed to load advertisements and for some features.
🔒 What data do you collect?
We do not collect personal data directly. Our ad partner Google AdMob may collect non-personal device data for ad targeting. See each app's Privacy Policy for full details.
🚫 How do I remove ads?
Ads help us keep our apps free. You can opt out of personalised ads from Settings → Google → Ads on your Android device.
💾 My progress was lost. What do I do?
Game progress is saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will delete saved progress. Contact us and we'll try our best to help.
